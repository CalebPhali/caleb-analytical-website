Chemical Analysis

At Caleb Analytical Solutions, our chemical analysis services are designed to deliver precise, dependable insights into the composition and structure of materials. Using advanced instrumentation and accredited methodologies, we provide quantitative and qualitative analysis tailored to your project requirements.

Our laboratory supports a wide range of industries, including mining, manufacturing, energy, and environmental science. We apply techniques such as ICP-OES, ICP-MS, XRF, XRD, GCMS, IC, BET, Bomb calorimetry, TGA, DSC, HPLC, AFM, and FTIR to evaluate everything from trace elements to bulk material content. Whether you're verifying raw material quality, testing final products, or ensuring regulatory compliance, our team ensures your data is accurate, reproducible, and timely.

Every analysis we conduct is supported by a rigorous QA/QC framework and interpreted by experienced chemists. We also assist clients in selecting the most appropriate techniques based on their objectives, material types, and detection limits.
